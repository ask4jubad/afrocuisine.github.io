African Cuisines and facts

Cuisines in African are as just varied as the continent. The origins of these African foods can be traced to locally grown farm produce. As in trend now, some popular African food are gaining recognition around the globe. This article will highlight some interesting facts that you may not be aware of about African cuisines

By tradition, these cuisines most often use a blend of local grains, fruits and vegetables, likewise meat and milk products. Cuisine differs from one country to another across the diverse tribe in Africa but there are certainly some resemblances among these cuisines due to long time effect of migration, trade and possibly marriages. Some facts below;


  • It will be interesting to know that fruits are one of most consumed in Africa. They include watermelon, bananas and coconuts. However, watermelon is popular as it grows natively in Africa.
  • Meat is also highly consumed as many of the important dishes in the continent are centered around meat of the meal. The attractiveness to meat was due to protein content and other nutrients that help to maintain the body energy as a result of massive heat while carrying out activities in the open.
  • Africans are great at producing organic foods. Several of the popular plants eaten around the world come from Africa. The difference in weather in various part of Africa makes it a suitable place for producing vegetables. Some of which are corn, yam, potatoes, cassava and okra.
